Hi there! I’m Santosh Gothwal
I support "Advancing Cancer Therapies for Every Life, from Genes to Justice."
Accomplished molecular biologist with deep expertise in human genetics, DNA repair, antibody generation, and cancer chemotherapeutic resistance. I’ve led cutting-edge research projects at top institutions like Osaka University and Kyoto University, under the mentorship of Nobel Laureate Prof. Tasuku Honjo.
Research Focus
Development of innovative approaches to identify novel cancer therapies.

Discovery and therapeutic application of intrinsic tumor suppressor mechanisms in Germinal center B cells.

Integration of clinical data with basic research to validate drug targets.
Professional Strengths
Exceptional research writing and technical troubleshooting.

Proven success in securing research funding.

Advanced skills in molecular biology, genetics, and biological methodologies.

Strong leadership in project development, safety, and management.
Join us in making a difference, one step at a time.
